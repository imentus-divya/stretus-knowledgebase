# Williams %R extremes

**Williams %R** is a bounded oscillator (commonly 0 to −100) similar in spirit to stochastic. Traders often treat readings near **0** as overbought and near **−100** as oversold, using **crosses** out of extremes as timing cues on swing charts.

## Overview

%R is **fast** and can generate many signals—filters are important.

## Signal definition

- **Oversold bounce cue**: %R moves **up** out of the oversold zone (e.g. below −80/−90).
- **Overbought fade cue**: %R moves **down** out of the overbought zone (e.g. above −20/−10).

Definitions differ by platform; verify your chart’s scaling.

## Parameters (typical)

| Parameter | Common values |
|-----------|----------------|
| Lookback | 14 |
| Zones | −20 / −80 (example) |

## How to interpret

In trends, %R can **oscillate** in one extreme—avoid fighting the regime without a plan.
