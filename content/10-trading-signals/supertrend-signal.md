# Supertrend signal

**Supertrend** is a popular overlay indicator (commonly ATR-based) that flips colour/state to suggest **bullish vs bearish** regimes. Many Indian intraday traders use it as a simple **stop-and-reverse** style signal on charts.

## Overview

Supertrend combines **ATR** with a multiplier to create a trailing threshold. When price closes on one side, the indicator flips—designed for **trend continuation** framing.

## Signal definition

- **Bullish regime**: indicator switches to the “buy” state (implementation-specific).
- **Bearish regime**: switches to the “sell” state.

Exact rules depend on platform defaults (ATR period, multiplier).

## Parameters (typical)

| Parameter | Common starting points |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| ATR period | 10–14 |
| Multiplier | 2–3 |

## How to interpret

Supertrend is **lagging** and can whipsaw in ranges. Consider using it with **higher timeframe bias** or **time filters** (e.g. avoid first/last minutes if spreads widen).
