# 9. Glossary — Indian Markets & Algo Trading

Abbreviations and terms used across the India knowledge base. **Verify** official definitions in SEBI/NSE/BSE documents for compliance use.

---

## A–C

**ASM (Additional Surveillance Measure)** — Exchange framework flagging securities for enhanced monitoring; may affect margins or trading mode.  
**BSE** — Bombay Stock Exchange.  
**Call auction** — Batch matching phase (e.g. pre-open) distinct from continuous trading.  
**Circuit / price band** — Daily price movement limit band for a security; can freeze trading or trigger auctions per rules.  
**CLOB** — Central limit order book; standard electronic order matching.  
**Co-location (colocation)** — Hosting trading systems in exchange data centre for low-latency access (regulated).  

---

## D–I

**F&O** — Futures and options segment.  
**FPI** — Foreign Portfolio Investor.  
**GSM (Graded Surveillance Measure)** — Surveillance framework often applied to securities with elevated risk characteristics; stages may impose restrictions.  
**ICCL** — Indian Clearing Corporation Limited (BSE clearing).  
**ISIN** — International Securities Identification Number.  
**IST** — Indian Standard Time (exchange sessions).  
**MTM** — Mark-to-market (daily P&L settlement on derivatives).  
**NSE** — National Stock Exchange of India.  
**NSCCL** — National Securities Clearing Corporation Limited (NSE clearing).  
**Nifty 50** — NSE flagship large-cap index (S&P BSE Sensex is BSE counterpart).  

---

## M–S

**Lot size** — Contract size for F&O; exchange revises periodically.  
**Peak margin** — Intraday margin reporting concept; brokers monitor client margin utilisation peaks.  
**Rolling settlement** — T+1 settlement cycle for equities (verify current rules).  
**SEBI** — Securities and Exchange Board of India.  
**SL / SL-M** — Stop-loss and stop-loss-market style orders (exact behaviour depends on broker/exchange product).  
**SPAN** — Standard Portfolio Analysis of Risk; portfolio margining for derivatives.  
**STT** — Securities Transaction Tax.  
**Tick size** — Minimum price increment for an instrument.  
**T+1** — Settlement one business day after trade date (for relevant segment).  
**VIX India** — India volatility index (conceptually implied volatility of index options).  

---

## Trading & algo

**Algo trading** — Automated generation/routing of orders subject to SEBI/broker frameworks; requires controls and identification.  
**DMA** — Direct market access (regulated).  
**Security master** — Database of tradable instruments and attributes.  
**Survivorship bias** — Backtest bias from using today’s universe instead of historical membership.  
**Impact cost** — Difference between decision price and realised average execution price.
